---
title: "aircraft_near_position"
description: "Find all live aircraft within a radius of a geographic position."
---

Returns all aircraft currently within a specified radius (in nautical miles) of a given latitude/longitude coordinate. The maximum search radius is 250 nautical miles.

## Parameters

<ParamField path="latitude" type="float" required>
  Latitude in decimal degrees. Must be between `-90` and `90`.
</ParamField>

<ParamField path="longitude" type="float" required>
  Longitude in decimal degrees. Must be between `-180` and `180`.
</ParamField>

<ParamField path="radius" type="float">
  Search radius in nautical miles. Default: `250`. Maximum: `250`. Must be greater than `0`.
</ParamField>

## Example queries

Ask Claude:
- "Show aircraft within 50nm of New York JFK"
- "What planes are near London Heathrow right now?"
- "Find aircraft within 100 nautical miles of 48.8566, 2.3522"

## Notes

<Tip>
  Use a smaller radius for more focused, faster results. A 50 nm radius around a major hub typically returns 20–80 aircraft.
</Tip>

<Note>
  Radius is in **nautical miles** (1 nm ≈ 1.852 km). Results are capped at 1000 aircraft per query.
</Note>

<Warning>
  Coordinates must be in decimal degrees format (e.g., `51.4775`), not degrees/minutes/seconds.
</Warning>
