---
title: "military_aircraft"
description: "Retrieve all live aircraft tagged as military."
---

Returns all aircraft currently airborne that are tagged as military in the airplanes.live database. No parameters required.

## Parameters

This tool takes no parameters.

## Example queries

Ask Claude:
- "What military aircraft are flying right now?"
- "Show me all military planes in the air"
- "List active military aircraft"

## Notes

<Note>
  Military aircraft coverage depends on what transponder data is available. Some military aircraft operate without transponders or with limited ADS-B output and will not appear.
</Note>

<Note>
  This data is provided for educational purposes only. Always refer to official sources for authoritative military flight information.
</Note>
